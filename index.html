The Process

How It Works

Six steps, totally transparent. No surprises.

  1. 01

    You message us

    WhatsApp, email, or fill out the form below. Tell us which spice, how much, and your country. We reply within a few hours.

  2. 02

    We send samples

    ₹500 sample fee (fully refunded on your first order). Shipped within 48 hours via courier so you can test before committing.

  3. 03

    Agree on price & packing

    We confirm the final price, packaging (bulk bags, branded boxes, private label), and delivery timeline together.

  4. 04

    50% upfront payment

    Wire transfer or LC. Once received, we begin procurement directly with our farm network.

  5. 05

    We load & ship

    Goods loaded at port — your name on all shipping documents. We send you the Bill of Lading, CO, and tracking details.

  6. 06

    Balance on delivery

    Pay the remaining 50% once the shipment lands. That's it — your spices are yours. No drama.
